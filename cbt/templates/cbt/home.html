Create computer based tests for yourself and your organnisation CBTMaker.

CBTMaker makes it easier for individuals and organnisations to create online computer tests(CBT), quizes and exams. Its practice session allows individuals to practice in order to improve their confidence before taking their actual tests.

Steps to create a computer-base test(CBT)

Creating a CBT requires sign up and log in. Ensure you do so.

  1. Create a CBT

    2. You start by creating a CBT which serves as the main container for your questions. Give your CBT a title, description, number of candidates and duration of test.

  2. Add questions

    3. After creating your CBT in step 1, go ahead to add questions to it depending on the number of choice.

  3. Add choice

    4. Add choices to your questions.
