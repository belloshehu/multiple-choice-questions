There are two types of Assessment you can create:

  1. Individual assessment
  2. Institution or organisation assessment

Assessments for Individual

Use this option to create quize, test and exams for students,children etc.

It is designed for teachers, parents, employers etc. who want to use this platform for their personal use.

Assessment for Institution

Use this option if you are creating test on behalf of an organisation, school or company.

This will require you to initially add the organisation you are creating the test during the creation process.

Steps to create a Assessment

Step 1

Create a CBT

You start by creating a CBT which serves as the main container for your questions. Give your CBT a title, description, number of candidates and duration of test.

Step 2

Add questions

After creating your CBT in step 1, go ahead to add questions to it depending on the number of choice.

Step 3

Add choice

Add choices to your questions.
