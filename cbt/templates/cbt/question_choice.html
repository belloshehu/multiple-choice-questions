Assessment question type

Question to be asked in assessment can be stand-alone or come from passage. On this note, there are two options for you to add questions to your assessment.

Choose the type of question you want to add to your assessment.

Questions from Passage

Question from passage example:

Below are examples of questions from passage

Read the passage carefully and use it to answer the questions that follow

Web development is one of the widest areas of software development. It is mainly categorised into front-end and backend. People taking care of the backend are called backend developers or engineers. while on the other, People taking care of the front-end are called front-end developers or engineers.

Each of these areas can be further categorised but in most cases, a dedicated developer handle each. People with knowledge of both the front-end and backend are refered to as fullstack developers.

Questions
  1. What are the major areas of the web development?
    2. Answers
    1. Frontend
    2. Backend
    3. Middle-End
    4. Non of the above

  2. Which of the available areas of web development is harder?
    3. Answers
    1. Frontend
    2. Backend
    3. Middle-End
    4. All of the above
